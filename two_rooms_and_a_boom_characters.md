## General Notes
- Every game must include **President** and **Bomber**.
- If a character description conflicts with general rules, the **card text wins**.
- Some characters require others to be in play.
- Teams: **Blue**, **Red**, **Grey**, **Green**, **Purple**, **Black**, **Pink**.

---

## Agent (Red/Blue)
**Power:** Agent (private reveal)  
Once per round, privately reveal to a player and force them to card share with you.

---

## Agoraphobe (Grey)
**Win Condition:** Never be sent to another room.

---

## Ahab (Grey)
**Win Condition:** Moby dies and you do not.  
**Requires:** Moby.

---

## Alchemist (Blue)
**Team Effect:** Blue Team loses unless the King card shares with the Alchemist.

---

## Alien (Red/Blue)
**Power:** Abduction  
Steal and keep cards from players who card share with you.

---

## Ambassador (Red/Blue)
Publicly revealed, immune, freely moves between rooms, cannot vote.

## Anarchist (Grey)

## Apprentice (Blue)
