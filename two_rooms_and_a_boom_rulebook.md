# Two Rooms and a Boom — Rulebook (v4.0)

**Designers:** Alan Gerding & Sean McCoy  
**Players:** 6–30  
**Playtime:** 7–20 minutes  
**Ages:** 8+

---

## Introduction (Basic Game)

*Two Rooms and a Boom* is a social deduction game with hidden roles and asymmetric win conditions.

There are **two teams**:
- **Blue Team** — protects the President
- **Red Team** — wants the Bomber to kill the President

Players are split between **two separate rooms**. Each player receives **one hidden character card**.

---

## How to Win

- **Red Team wins** if the **Bomber** is in the same room as the **President** at game end.
- **Blue Team wins** if the President survives.
- **Gambler** wins by correctly predicting which team won.

---

## Components

- Rulebook  
- 2 Leader cards (one per room)  
- Character cards:
  - President
  - Bomber
  - Red Team cards
  - Blue Team cards
  - Gambler (odd player counts)
- Stopwatch (not included)

---

## Setup

1. Build the character deck:
   - President
   - Bomber
   - Equal number of Red & Blue Team cards
   - Add Gambler if odd number of players

2. Split players evenly into two rooms.

3. Deal each player one face-down character card.

4. Start the timer:
   - Round 1: 3 minutes

---

## Game Rounds

The game has **three rounds**:
1. 3 minutes
2. 2 minutes
3. 1 minute

Each round ends with a **hostage exchange** between rooms.

---

## The Three Core Rules

1. **Stay in your room**
2. **No communication between rooms**
3. **No trading character cards**

You *may* reveal your card, partially or fully, but you may not swap cards.

---

## Leaders & Hostages

### Leader
- One leader per room
- Leaders select hostages at the end of each round
- Leaders can **never** be hostages

### Hostage Counts

| Players | Round 1 | Round 2 | Round 3 |
|------|-------|-------|-------|
| 6–10 | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| 11–21 | 2 | 1 | 1 |
| 22+ | 3 | 2 | 1 |

---

## Choosing a Leader

- First nomination = leader
- Leader takes the leader card
- Leaders can:
  - **Abdicate** (hand off card)
  - Be **usurped** by majority vote

---

## End of Each Round

1. **Hostage Selection** (announced privately to room)
2. **Parlay** (leaders meet between rooms, timer resets)
3. **Hostage Exchange**

Hostage choices may not change once announced.

---

## End of the Game

After the final hostage exchange:
- All players reveal cards
- Bomber detonates
- Resolve win conditions

Optional: Gather players together *after* room resolution.

---

## Gambler

If included:
- Gambler publicly predicts winning team at game end
- Correct = win
- Incorrect = loss

Room location does not matter.

---

## Advanced Game Overview

The advanced game introduces:
1. **Advanced Character Cards**
2. **Additional Rounds** (up to 5 total)

Advanced character rules override basic rules when in conflict.

---

## Advanced Terminology

- **Allegiance** – team color
- **Card Share** – full consensual card exchange
- **Color Share** – consensual color-only reveal
- **Private Reveal** – one-way reveal
- **Public Reveal** – reveal to multiple players
- **Condition** – status applied to players
- **Cleanse** – removes all conditions
- **Buried Card** – out-of-play card
- **Power** – mandatory effect tied to character

## Doctor & Engineer Special Rules

These characters add **additional win conditions**:

### Doctor
- President must card share with Doctor
- Failure = Red Team wins

### Engineer
- Bomber must card share with Engineer
- Failure = Blue Team wins

Only the **current** Bomber and President matter at game end.
